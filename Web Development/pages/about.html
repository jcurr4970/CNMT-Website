What We Do

At Everybody's closet, our mission is to provide second hand clothing at a discounted price. We believe that every person has a right to cool and unique clothing. We strive to only accept the clothing we would want to wear. All clothing comes from donations from the community. Our locations can be found in smaller towns that don't have a consignment store available. Not only do we provide clothes, we also have sporting goods. We have games for children, puzzles, and books. We are here to provide a service some places don't have, but desperatly need.

About our staff

Our staff is one of the best around. We work with local schools to provide jobs to teens in school. We also work with local hiring agencies to find candidates around communities. Our staff are trained in customer serivce and appreciation. Our staff enjoy the discounts they receive while working with us. We aim to provide a stable and consistent working environment to allow employees to strive to do their best.
