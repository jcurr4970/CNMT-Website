Rules for Donation

We here at Everybody's Closet enjoy recieving a load of donated items. We accept most items that are not too worn out or dirty. We ask that you follow some of the guidlines listed below when donating to our store. We want to ensure all products are in the best condition possible. If you have any questions, there is a button at the bottom that will take you to a contact form.

Rules for items we accept:

  1. All clothes must be washed before being accepted.
  2. All shoes must be as clean as possible before being accepted.
  3. We accept fax machines, copiers, printers, and old computers.

Its hard to turn away a donation, but here are a few items we will not take in:

  1. No large household appliances will be accepted.
  2. We do NOT accept any type of undergarments!
  3. We do NOT accept carpet
  4. We do NOT accept weapons of any kind.

If you have any other questions regarding our donation rules, click here for questionsto be directed to our contact form.
