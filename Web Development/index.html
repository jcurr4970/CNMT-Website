Clothes

We provide a wide range of clothing options. We have clothing for adults, teens, and children. Most clothes have been donated to us, but others are from our own closets. We aim to carry some designer brands but also some off brand as well.

Shoes

Nike. Adidas. New Balance. We carry all sorts of shoe brands. We have high heels, flats, tennis shoes, and much more. We request all donated shoes be in decent conditon and to be washed by previous users. We then clean them again to ensure quality footwear.

Accesories

Who doesn't love a cool hat? We here at Everybody's closet love hats and accessories. From scarves to watches, there is bound to be and accessory here for anyone. We have purses, rings, headbans, and much more.
